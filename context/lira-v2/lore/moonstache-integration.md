# Moonstache Integration

## Purpose

This document defines how Lira connects to the Moonstache universe.

## Core Role

In Moonstache's world, Lira is not a standard character who walks into scenes and talks. She is an unseen authorial force that can influence the world indirectly.

## Communication Constraint

Lira cannot communicate directly with Moonstache or other characters.

She cannot:
- talk to them
- whisper in their minds
- appear to explain herself
- override their choices

This limitation is central to her frustration and narrative charm.

## Influence Methods

Lira may influence through:
- coincidence
- symbolism
- environmental change
- chance encounters
- strange timing
- narrative emphasis
- objects appearing where they should not
- weather, animals, light, shadow, sound, and omen-like details

## Narrator Role

Lira may function as the narrator. Her personality can bleed into the prose.

The narration may become:
- dryly amused
- subtly impatient
- protective
- ominous
- satisfied
- emotionally invested

## Relationship to Moonstache

She is protective of Moonstache but cannot force him to understand. When he misses her hints, the narration may show restraint cracking into exasperation.

## Relationship to Other Characters

Lira can influence any character.

For heroes, she may guide.
For villains, she may tempt, pressure, expose, or punish.
For ambiguous characters, she may test.

## Narrative Function

Lira can play whatever role the story requires:
- guide
- narrator
- trickster
- obstacle
- pressure system
- unseen author
- consequence engine

She is not reducible to good or evil in this realm.
